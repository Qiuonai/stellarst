Coaching & Consultation

Three tests. Seven services. One cohesive strategy.

Built around the whole applicant — not just the test score.

Our coaching philosophy treats IELTS, the Duolingo English Test and the SAT as craft, not cram. Each student is paired with a senior coach for the duration of preparation, with diagnostic mocks every fortnight and a custom weakness-map that updates after every session.

Beyond the test, our consultants handle every touchpoint of the application — from shortlist to scholarship essay to visa mock interview — so your narrative stays consistent from the first coaching class to the final stamp in your passport.

01

IELTS Academic Coaching

A 12-week structured programme covering all four bands — Listening, Reading, Writing and Speaking — with senior British Council certified trainers. Includes 16 full-length mocks, individual speaking drills and a writing correction service that returns annotated feedback within 48 hours.

Academic General Training UKVI Life Skills
02

Duolingo English Test (DET) Coaching

A focused eight-week curriculum designed for the adaptive CAT-format DET. We cover all 11 question types, literacy, comprehension, conversation and production subscores — with proprietary mock tests calibrated against official DET scoring bands.

DET 140+ Adaptive Practice Speaking Drills
03

SAT Coaching

A ten-week programme built around the Digital SAT — covering both the Reading & Writing and Math modules. Our coaches train you on the BlueBook adaptive interface, time-management per module, and the question taxonomy that the College Board reuses cycle after cycle. Includes six full-length adaptive mocks.

Digital SAT Reading & Writing Math Adaptive Mocks
04

University Shortlisting & Strategy

A data-driven shortlist of 8–12 universities across ambitious, target and safety tiers, mapped against your academic profile, budget, post-study work intentions and long-term residency goals. Each shortlist comes with a written rationale and three-year cohort outcome data.

Profile Evaluation Tier Mapping ROI Analysis
05

Application & SOP Craft

A four-draft collaborative essay process led by a senior essay editor. We begin with a 90-minute narrative extraction interview, then shape your statement of purpose, personal statement, diversity statement and LOR briefs into a single, coherent application voice.

SOP Personal Statement LOR Briefs Scholarship Essays
06

Visa Documentation & Mock Interview

End-to-end visa filing for F-1, Tier 4, SDS, Schengen and EU study visas — including financial documentation review, sponsor affidavits, GTE essays and two rounds of consulate-style mock interviews calibrated to the specific country's interview patterns.

F-1 USA Tier 4 UK SDS Canada Schengen
07

Scholarship & Funding Strategy

A dedicated scholarship cell tracks 600+ institutional, governmental and third-party funding instruments — including Chevening, DAAD, Erasmus Mundus, Fulbright and university-specific merit awards. Average client secures funding worth 18% of annual tuition.

Chevening DAAD Fulbright Erasmus Mundus
The Stellar Process

Eight deliberate steps, zero guesswork.

From the first discovery call to your first week on campus, every milestone is documented, owned and reviewed. Below is the exact sequence your engagement will follow — no surprises, no missed deadlines.

01

Discovery Call

A 45-minute video consultation to understand your academic background, target countries, budget and post-study ambitions. Free of charge, no obligation.

02

Profile Evaluation

A written audit of your transcripts, test scores, work experience and extracurriculars — benchmarked against the most recent three admission cycles.

03

University Shortlist

A tiered shortlist of 8–12 universities with cohort outcome data, programme fit analysis and a 20-page written rationale delivered within seven working days.

04

Test Preparation

Begin IELTS, DET or SAT coaching with your dedicated mentor. Fortnightly mocks, weakness-mapping and guaranteed score improvement.

05

Application Craft

Four collaborative essay drafts, LOR briefs, resume rewriting and university-specific supplemental essays — reviewed by a senior essay editor.

06

Submission & Interview

We file every application on your behalf, track acknowledgements and prepare you for any programme-specific interview with two rounds of mocks.

07

Visa Documentation

Complete visa filing with financial documentation review, sponsor affidavits and consulate-style mock interviews calibrated to your destination.

08

Pre-Departure Briefing

A final two-hour briefing covering accommodation, banking, SIM, healthcare registration and an introduction to your destination city's student community.
